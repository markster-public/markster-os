# Company Identity

## One-Sentence Definition

Markster installs and runs the sales and marketing system most service firms never had.

## Category

- primary category: Managed Revenue Operations
- investor framing: Service-as-Software, revenue operations vertical
- categories we reject: digital marketing agency, consulting, SaaS platform

## What We Do

- We build, run, and measure a complete sales and marketing system for owner-operated service firms
- Clients receive an operating outreach machine: contacts identified, emails sequenced, pipeline tracked, performance reported
- Their business gets predictable new client flow without hiring, without vendors, and without building it themselves

## What We Do Not Do

- We do not do branding, logo design, or creative services
- We do not sell software licenses or training-only engagements
- We do not hand off a playbook and leave

## Why We Exist

Most service firms stall because the commercial function runs on the founder. That pattern is the same at every scale. We build the system that removes the founder as the bottleneck.

## Strategic Position

- done-for-you execution at a price point accessible to 5-50 person firms
- we own the outcome, not just the tool
- we accept lower margin for higher accountability

## Known Identity Risks

- People assume we are a typical marketing agency. We are not. We own the system.
- Confusing "managed" with "outsourced" - we build internal capability, not dependency
- The AI angle is a delivery mechanism, not the product promise
