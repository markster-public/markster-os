# Audience

## Primary ICP

- title or role: owner/managing partner at an accounting or CPA firm
- company type: accounting firm, CPA practice, tax advisory
- company size: 2-50 employees
- geography: US-based
- trigger situation: plateaued on referrals, wants more clients without adding headcount

## Secondary ICP

- B2B professional service firm (law, consulting, financial advisory) with the same growth plateau problem

## Buying Triggers

- referral pipeline drying up or unpredictable
- lost a major client and need to replace revenue
- wants to grow but does not want to hire a marketing person
- competitor is growing and they are not

## Pain Points

- operational: no system for consistent outreach; everything is ad hoc
- financial: revenue growth is flat or tied directly to founder's time
- emotional: tired of starting from scratch every quarter; wants something that runs without them

## Desired Outcomes

- a full pipeline that does not depend on referrals or their personal network
- more time on client work and less on chasing new business
- a system that can run even when they are not looking at it

## Objections

- "We've tried agencies before and it didn't work"
- "Our clients come from referrals, outbound doesn't work for us"
- "I'm too busy to onboard something new right now"
- "What makes this different from what we already tried"

## Current Alternatives

- doing nothing (status quo, waiting for referrals)
- hiring a part-time marketing coordinator
- using a generic marketing agency
- LinkedIn manual outreach by the partner

## Buyer Language

- "We get most of our clients through referrals"
- "I don't have time to do marketing myself"
- "I've tried cold email before and it didn't work"
- "I want more clients but not at the cost of hiring another person"
- "We're too small for the big firms but too big to keep doing this manually"

## Disqualifiers

- companies under $300K revenue (no budget for the offer)
- founders who want to do it themselves and just need a playbook
- companies that have an active, functioning marketing team
