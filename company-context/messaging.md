# Messaging

## One-Liner

We install and run the sales and marketing system most service firms never had.

## Short Pitch

Most accounting firms get their clients through referrals. When that stops working, there is no system to fall back on. We build and run the outbound system so the firm generates consistent new client conversations without the principal doing it manually.

## Problem Framing

- what is broken today: most accounting firms have no outbound system; new clients depend entirely on referrals and the principal's network
- what it costs: growth is capped at what the founder can personally manage; one slow quarter can trigger real cash flow stress
- why now: the firms that build a predictable client acquisition system now will outpace the ones still waiting for referrals in 12 months

## Differentiators

- we own the execution, not just the strategy or the tools
- the system is built, running, and reporting before we ask for the second month's payment
- we have a verified accounting industry case study (Robert, $443K to $900K in 73 days)
- we remove the principal as the bottleneck instead of adding to their workload

## Key Claims

- claim: revenue doubled for a CPA client in 73 days
- proof status: verified
- approved usage notes: Robert, A1 Accounting, California; use first name only; do not quote a revenue multiple publicly

- claim: principal work hours cut from 70 to 33 per week in the same engagement
- proof status: verified
- approved usage notes: same source; strong hook for overworked principals

- claim: done-for-you execution with no team to hire and no tools to configure
- proof status: approved description of the offer
- approved usage notes: all channels

## Message Variants

- founder-to-founder: "I've run growth at every scale. The failure mode is the same everywhere. I build the fix and measure the outcome."
- buyer-facing: "We install and run your sales and marketing system. You approve the strategy. We handle everything else."
- partner-facing: not currently defined for CPA vertical
- investor-facing: Managed Revenue Operations; Service-as-Software; $7,500 setup + $8,500/month; ICP: 2-50 person professional service firms

## Banned Phrases

- "revenue system" - use "sales and marketing system"
- "growth department" - use "growth system"
- "14 days" - not proven for new clients
- "9.2x" - wrong number
- "AI-powered" leading - AI is the mechanism, not the promise
- any em-dash or en-dash in external copy

## Required Anchors

- "sales and marketing system" (not "revenue system")
- "done-for-you"
- "no team to hire"
- "we own the execution"
- the Robert case study result (when speaking to accounting prospects)
