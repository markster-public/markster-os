# Offer

## Core Offer

- offer name: The Growth Install
- what is included: full build and management of the outbound sales and marketing system (contacts, sequences, CRM, reporting)
- who it is for: owner-operated accounting firms and professional service businesses with 2-50 employees

## Entry Offer

- low-friction first step: discovery call or AI Grader assessment
- price: not shown on most pages
- timeline: system live within the first weeks of engagement
- deliverable: operating outreach pipeline with weekly reporting

## Outcome Statement

Clients get a working outbound system that generates new client conversations without adding headcount or handing it off to a vendor.

Results vary by market, list quality, and engagement length. The Robert case study (accounting, California) went from $443K to $900K in 73 days while cutting principal hours from 70 to 33 per week.

## Pricing Logic

- pricing model: one-time setup ($7,500) plus monthly management ($8,500/month)
- what drives price up: complexity of offer, multi-partner firms, custom sequences
- what drives price down: not currently discounted; pricing reflects execution ownership
- what is intentionally not optimized for: volume of leads, vanity metrics

## Risk Reversal

- no performance guarantee is made in writing
- confidence mechanism: discovery call is structured to disqualify bad fits before any commitment
- if fit is unclear: we do not take the engagement

## Delivery Boundaries

- what we do: prospect identification, email sequences, CRM management, reporting, outreach execution
- what we need from the client: 30-minute weekly check-in, approval of messaging before launch
- what is excluded: social media management, paid ads, content marketing, PR

## Upgrade Paths

- next logical offer: expansion into additional verticals or geographies
- expansion path: ScaleOS self-managed layer for operators who want to own the system themselves
