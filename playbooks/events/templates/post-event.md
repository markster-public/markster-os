# Post-Event Follow-Up Template

Send within 48 hours. Reference your specific conversation. One clear next step.

Variables:
- `[FIRST_NAME]` - their first name
- `[EVENT_NAME]` - name of the event
- `[CONVERSATION_DETAIL]` - the specific thing you discussed or they mentioned
- `[RELEVANT_RESOURCE]` - something useful you can share (article, template, intro, case study) - optional but strong
- `[NEXT_STEP]` - what you offered to do next (call, send something, make an intro)
- `[YOUR_NAME]` - your name

---

## Version A: After a strong conversation with a clear next step

**Subject:** Good to meet at [EVENT_NAME]

---

[FIRST_NAME],

Good to meet at [EVENT_NAME]. Enjoyed our conversation about [CONVERSATION_DETAIL].

As I mentioned, [NEXT_STEP - e.g., "I'll send you that case study on the MSP segment" or "happy to set up that intro with [THIRD_PARTY]"].

[If sharing something: here it is: [LINK or brief summary]]

Worth a 20-minute call to continue from where we left off?

[CALENDAR_LINK or two time options]

[YOUR_NAME]

---

## Version B: After a lighter conversation (no explicit next step agreed)

**Subject:** [EVENT_NAME] follow-up

---

[FIRST_NAME],

Good to connect briefly at [EVENT_NAME]. [CONVERSATION_DETAIL - brief reference to what you talked about].

[RELEVANT_RESOURCE if applicable: "Thought this was relevant to what you mentioned - [link or 1-sentence description]."]

I work with [ICP type] on [OUTCOME in one sentence]. Happy to share more if it's relevant to what you're working on.

[YOUR_NAME]

---

## Version C: Second touch (3-5 days after Version B, no reply)

**Subject:** Re: [EVENT_NAME] follow-up

---

[FIRST_NAME],

Following up in case my last note got buried.

[One sentence referencing the conversation again - keep it specific.]

Worth a quick call?

[YOUR_NAME]

---

## Notes on post-event follow-up

- Send within 48 hours. The earlier you send, the higher the reply rate.
- Lead with something specific to your conversation, not a generic "great meeting you."
- The offer of a relevant resource (article, intro, tool) increases reply rate significantly if the resource is genuinely useful and not just a sales pitch.
- Every follow-up should move toward a specific next step: a call, a meeting, or a clear "not now." Vague follow-ups generate vague outcomes.
