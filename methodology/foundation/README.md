# Foundation: F1-F4

Foundation is the prerequisite layer of ScaleOS. Four questions answered systematically before any channel execution begins.

Companies skip Foundation because it feels like planning instead of doing. The result is campaigns that don't resonate, offers that don't close, and messages that sound like everyone else in the category.

Foundation locks your targeting, pricing logic, message structure, and channel sequence. It takes 3-5 hours total. It saves months.

---

## The four steps

**[F1: ICP](F1-icp.md)** - Who exactly are you selling to, and why do they buy now?
The ICP is not a demographic. It is a specific company profile with a specific buying trigger. Two companies with identical revenue and headcount can have completely different conversion rates based on situation alone.

**[F2: Offer](F2-offer.md)** - What outcome are you selling, and at what price?
Buyers do not buy deliverables. They buy outcomes. An offer defined around outcomes is easier to price, easier to sell, and harder to compare against competitors.

**[F3: Message](F3-message.md)** - How do you describe the problem in their words?
Message structure matters: problem (in their language) -> mechanism (why now) -> proof (specific, verifiable). Most companies skip directly to the mechanism and lose the buyer in the first sentence.

**[F4: Channel](F4-channel.md)** - Where do you reach your ICP first, given everything you know about them?
Channel selection is not a preference. It is a function of your ICP's buying behavior, deal size, and trust requirements. This step tells you which motion to activate first.

## How to use these files

Each file contains:
1. The question the step answers
2. Why it matters (what breaks if you skip it)
3. The framework for answering it
4. Specific questions to answer in writing
5. A summary template to document your output

Your completed answers become the context that every skill uses. When you run `/cold-email` or `/sales`, the skill asks for your Foundation outputs first.
